\uppercase{\textbf{The proposed segmentation model}}
  comprehends the following six homogeneous regions, in hierarchical order:
  1) thalamus \mytcbox[thalamus]{$\Omega_{Tha}$};
  2) ventricular system and deep gray matter structures \mytcbox[ventricles_gm]{$\Omega_{VdGM}$};
  3) cerebral white matter \mytcbox[wm]{$\Omega_{WM}$};
  4) brain stem and cerebellar white matter \mytcbox[brainstem]{$\Omega_{bst}$};
  5) cerebellar gray matter \mytcbox[cerebellar_gm]{$\Omega_{cbGM}$}; and
  6) cortical gray matter \mytcbox[gm]{$\Omega_{GM}$}.

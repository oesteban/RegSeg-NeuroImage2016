\uppercase{\textbf{The proposed segmentation model}}
  comprehends the following six homogeneous regions, in hierarchical order:
  1) thalamus \mytcbox[thalamus]{$\Omega_\text{Tha}$};
  2) ventricular system and deep gray matter structures \mytcbox[ventricles_gm]{$\Omega_\text{VdGM}$};
  3) cerebral white matter \mytcbox[wm]{$\Omega_\text{WM}$};
  4) brain stem and cerebellar white matter \mytcbox[brainstem]{$\Omega_\text{bst}$};
  5) cerebellar gray matter \mytcbox[cerebellar_gm]{$\Omega_\text{cbGM}$}; and
  6) cortical gray matter \mytcbox[gm]{$\Omega_\text{GM}$}.
